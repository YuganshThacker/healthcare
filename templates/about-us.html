About Us

Healthstack is an online platform developed as part of our Software Group Project (SGP) by Yugansh, Aditya, Denish, and Yash. It is designed to support healthcare systems by providing urgent medical assistance to patients in need of emergency care. The platform enables seamless tracking, monitoring, and sharing of patient health records across connected hospitals. Patients can also view information about various healthcare providers and book appointments online with ease.
